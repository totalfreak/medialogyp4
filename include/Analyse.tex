\chapter{Analysis}

\section{Analysis Intro}
In this analysis, the focus will be on the investigation of the current issues with the fundamentals of musical education in elementary schools.\\
\\
Furthermore, current tools and technological inventions will be investigated to incorporate the same aspects to a final prototype.

\section{Problem Area}
- Based on interviews and observations done with teachers and students\\
- Issues in a musical classroom\\
- Current tools, methods and the issues which is current utilizing these resources\\
- Possible investigations done beforehand and their results
\section{Target Group}
- Elementary school children (Grade 1,2,3,4,5,6)\\
- Potentially the teachers as a sub target group?
